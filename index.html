The 4I Lab was established to conduct applied research in engineering technologies relevant to industrial and societal tasks. It houses state-of-the-art design and rapid prototyping equipment. The four ‘I’s stand for Ideation, Innovation, Incubation and Implementation. The 4I Lab encourages and promotes innovation keeping it at the forefront of developments. The people of the 4I Lab are driven to serve and support each other and inspire others to innovate further.
